Pase 2 of 4 (Prob more)

Art and UI Design Update

Our artists have been hard at work updating the UI design and sprite assets. In the previous phase, our UI was getting a little cluttered which made it difficult for future updates or additions to the UI. Some of the art assets are being updated to fit a variety of aspect ratios at the moment as well. Along with this update, there was the implementation of a visual UI element for blood need.

Code Restructuring and Planning

We have done a restructure of our code. We have made our "player" class a manager class that is implemented using the singleton design pattern (safe with unity) with future plans of incorporating thread safe capabilities. Inventory and item classes were improved heavily to make expanding their concepts in future updates easier.

The team is currently testing or working on these concepts for future updates:

  • Priority Queue for Crop Growth Progress
  • Animations for Crop Growth Progress and Completion
  • Observer Pattern for Events and Order Completions
  • Upgrade, Harvest, etc. UI menu and mechanics for strucutres.

Placing Structures

Structures can now be placed upon the grid through a drag and drop as well as removed. Currently there is testing in place for work regarding user friendly model for removal and moving.

Structure Shop

Structures that can be placed on the grid are now available for purchase. Structures can be bought and placed as it updates inventory and currentcy.

What's Next?

Next steps would be to continue planning and testing for future updates regarding in progress mechanics and additions. The team has a focus on crop harvesting and planting. Next step would be to implement orders and also adding new resources and structures past the wheat crop and the town homes.
